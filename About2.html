About Me

My name is Kevin Wang and I was born and raised in Southern California. For as long as I can remember, programming and technology have interested me greatly. I am currently a member of the UC Berkeley Class of 2019 as an aspiring Computer Science major.

My passions include camping and competitive swimming. I have been involved with the Boy Scouts of America since I was eight and have earned the rank of Eagle Scout. I have attended swimming clubs since I was six and started swimming competitively when I was ten. Although I no longer swim in college, I love anything that has to do with water and I am also a certified lifeguard.
